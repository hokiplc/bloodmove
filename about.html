About

Part of Polar Thermal Packaging Ltd

bloodmove is not a new company — it's the blood transport vertical within Polar Thermal Packaging Ltd, a UK manufacturer based in Warminster, Wiltshire, brought together here for clarity and visibility.

Polar Thermal's entry into thermal control began with a Ministry of Defence request to prevent frozen food from melting at dockside. Under CEO Paul Harrison's leadership, the team expanded into temperature-controlled transport packaging for major clients including the National Blood Service, DHL, Marks & Spencer, Tesco, Sainsbury's, AstraZeneca and Pfizer — with products deployed globally, including conflict zones such as Afghanistan.
