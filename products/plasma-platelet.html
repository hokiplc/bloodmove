Inbound · PPC

Plasma & Platelet Container

Platelet & plasma transport from donation centres to regional hubs. Developed for NHS Blood and Transplant (NHSBT), the bloodmove Inbound PPC has been used for over 8 years with integrated PCM protection.

  • Integrated phase change materials for reliable, compliant performance
  • Maintains controlled payload temperature for a minimum of 4 hours
  • Performs across -5°C to +35°C ambient temperatures
  • 100% validation score across multiple validation exercises
