Flagship system

TriTemp

One system for three blood temperature profiles. The bloodmove TriTemp System is a validated, temperature-controlled transport solution for the safe movement of Red Blood Cells, Platelets and Frozen Plasma within a single transport platform.

  • Validated for three distinct blood temperature profiles in one platform
  • Stackable design for efficient loading and storage
  • Developed from 8+ years of NHSBT-validated thermal engineering
  • Reusable, manufactured in-house in the UK
